From a mathematics foundation at IIT Madras to architecting AI in production — I build the reliable machinery that turns models into measurable outcomes.

My work sits at the intersection of applied ML, LLM systems, and real infrastructure. I care less about demos and more about what survives production: cost, latency, observability, and trust.
